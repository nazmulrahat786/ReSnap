⚠️ Top 5 Mistakes Students Make in Admission Forms

  • Wrong photo size or format — Always check the pixel and file size requirements.
  • Misspelled names — Ensure your name matches academic records exactly.
  • Wrong GPA calculations — Use official guidelines and tools to calculate GPA.
  • Incorrect quota selection — Select quotas only if you're eligible and provide proof.
  • Late submission — Submit your form early to avoid server crashes or missed deadlines.

✨ Extra Tips for a Smooth Admission:

  • Keep digital copies of all required documents ready in the correct format.
  • Use a reliable internet connection when filling out and submitting forms.
  • Read instructions twice — don't rush through important fields.
  • Take screenshots or save PDFs of your submission confirmation.
  • If unsure, ask your school or the admission help desk before guessing.

✅ Pro Tip: Double-check everything before submitting your form. Small errors can cost you big opportunities!
